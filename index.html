The term elemental transmogrification is inspired by the Transmogrifier, an invention from Calvin and Hobbes that would turn one thing into another. In this exhibition, each installation blends fundamental aspects of traditional art forms with digital technology, allowing one form to transform into another. Users are invited to interact with each installation, lending a hand in the transformation of physical to digital, 2D to 3D, visual to auditory, tangible to intangible.

About Me

I'm Laura, a tinkerer who gets excited about the interaction of art and technology. With a background in mechanical engineering, programming, and art, I like to experiment with mixing mediums and see what is created!
